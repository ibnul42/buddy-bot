Welcome to BuddyBot

A chatbot designed to interact with teenagers who are between the ages of 15 and 18 years old and need help navigating their isolation-related stress and mental health disorders.

Why BuddyBot

Millions of Americans grapple with mental illness each year, and more than 50% of them go untreated. The number of high-tech virtual and app-based Chatbot startups offering virtual therapy and messaging therapy sessions is on the rise, due to the exponential growth of virtual medicine, which facilitated the advancement and accessibility of mental health services in recent years.

Isolation

Isolation is a significant detrimental factor in the development of teenagers and their future behavior as adults. Isolation can cause multiple problems amongst teenage students, and the resulting lack of social interaction disposes this vulnerable demographic to a number of mental health disorders.

Bullying

Bullying is the repeated psychological or physical persecution that is commonly, but not always, inflicted upon a weaker person by a stronger individual and always includes the intent to harm the victim. Bullying among schoolchildren is fairly common; in some studies, about half of the children were bullies, and more than half were victims.

Importance

Mental health disorders afflicting teenagers and young adults must be tackled as early as possible to prevent them from evolving into more severe mental/physical and behavioral illnesses that persist into adulthood. Half of all mental health symptoms start by 14 years of age, but most cases go undetected and untreated, according to the World Health Organization.
