Welcome to BuddyBot!

A chatbot designed to interact with teenagers who are between the ages of 15 and 18 years old and need help navigating their isolation-related stress and mental health disorders. BuddyBot is a conversational mental health virtual confidante that converses with teens from the comfort, privacy, and convenience of their own homes. Specifically, this bot tackles the effects of isolation users faced during the Covid-19 pandemic, and it focuses on isolation which is caused by both real-life and virtual bullying. The number of high-tech virtual and app-based chatbot startups offering virtual therapy sessions are on the rise, due to the exponential growth of virtual medicine. BuddyBot strives to be a vital part of the movement by specifically appealing to young individuals and making them feel heard. Activate your experience with BuddyBot by pressing the blue chat button!
